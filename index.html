June 29, 2025 NRI Pulse

Student-Led Platform ElevateGen Launched to Empower Future Leaders in Forsyth County

A dynamic new student-led initiative, ElevateGen, has launched in Forsyth County with the goal of helping students turn ambition into action. Created by a group of young leaders in partnership with Idealogy Media, ElevateGen is a platform designed to bridge the gap between education and real-world experience for high school and college students, as well as early-career seekers.

June 29, 2025 Atlanta Dunia

Career GPS: How ElevateGen Helps Students Navigate What's Next

A group of young leaders in Forsyth County have started ElevateGen, a new student-led platform designed to assist high school students, college students, and early career seekers in developing professional skills, exploring career paths, and gaining real-world experience in an era where students are expected to navigate their future without the necessary tools or experience.
